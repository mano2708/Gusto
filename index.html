Government College of Engineering - Erode

The college established in 1984, is a premier Engineering Institute. It was run by All Transport Corporations of Government of TamilNadu and now the Institute is converted as Government College of Engineering, Erode(GCEE). The Institute is located on a sprawling lush green campus of 200 acres between Chithode and Bhavani in Erode district. It provides a congenial atmosphere for Technical Education. The Institute has excellent infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, library and highly qualified & experienced faculty members. The Institute is well known for its technical excellence, modern facilities, research activities, good academic performance track record and placements.

Formal Team Events

PROJECT PRESENTATION

Having an eagerness on the plan of thoughts into an action of real time projects.The interpretation of ideas are more interactive and to extend a knowledge and network with fellow collegues.Let's get together on innovations.

RULES AND REGULATIONS :

  • The participants could be solo or a team of two/three.
  • The participants must upload their project abstract, along with existing system, proposed solutions with methodology, and scope, maximum of 5 pages as softcopy during registration.
  • The participants must bring their working project model and presentation slides.
  • Presentation will approximately take 5-10 minutes per team, followed by live demonstration of the project.
  • The participants must provide the project report as a hardcopy.
  • Batches will be allocated based on the registration.
  • The winners will be determined by juries.
  • Contestants who violate the rules and guidelines will be eliminated instantly.

PAPER PRESENTATION

Have a keen knowledge of urging an useful presentation. Let's make enthusiasm of presenting an opportunity to showcase Engineering Research skills ,Innovative ideas and Talents.Engage this platform to expand knowledge and network with fellow collegues

RULES AND REGULATIONS :

  • Team participation is allowed, minimum two and maximum of three members.
  • Participants must submit abstracts of their papers during the registration process.
  • Papers will be shortlisted based on the quality, relevance, and originality of the abstract.
  • Authors of the shortlisted papers will be notified of acceptance via email.
  • Accepted authors will receive instructions on how to proceed with full paper submission and registration.
  • Authors of accepted papers must prepare and deliver an oral presentation for 10 minutes on stage performance and may include visual aids such as PowerPoint slides and posters followed by Q&A.
  • Winning contestants will be rewarded with cash prizes.

CODE DEBUGGING

Get ready to untangle the web of bugs!Join the Debugging Marathon and let's turn error messages into triumphs. It's time to flex those coding and celebrate the victorious dance of a bug-free script!

RULES AND REGULATIONS :

  • Code debugging event with provided code.

HUNT MODS

Join the Debugging Marathon! Unravel bugs into triumphs. Flex your coding muscles and dance to the victory of bug-free scripts! Let's untangle the web and celebrate flawless code!

RULES AND REGULATIONS :

ROUND 1 RULES: 30 MINS

  • The code has the logical error in it, the participants need to alter the logic
  • And provide the output mentioned.
  • Preferred programming language: C, Python, Java
  • The program must be run only once, with proctoring by volunteers.
  • Participants can run the code 'n' times within the allocated time.

ROUND 2 RULES: 30 MINS

  • The difficulty level of the question will be set to medium.
  • Participants can run the code 'n' times within the allocated time.
  • Time is everything for the event, no extended time for participants.
  • The participant to successfully run the program (based on time) or provide the best output will be declared the winner of the contest.
